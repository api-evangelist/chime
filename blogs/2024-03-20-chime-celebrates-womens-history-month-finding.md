The ChimeHers resource group explored varied definitions of success with female and non-binary employees, framing success as process-focused rather than destination-focused and emphasizing integrity and personal growth. Participants discussed how life changes such as parenthood and career shifts reshape success, with influences including family values, mentors, and philosophies like Ikigai.
