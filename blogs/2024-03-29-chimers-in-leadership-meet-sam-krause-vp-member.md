Sam Krause, VP of Member Strategy & Experience Operations, discovered her passion for developing others' potential as a college program supervisor leading volunteers. Her leadership philosophy centers on setting bold visions and empowering teams with autonomy, while balancing near-term and long-term progress. She acknowledges leadership's isolating aspects and recommends building peer support networks.
