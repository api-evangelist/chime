Brian Mullins, SVP of Product, leads product management and operations for Chime's Foundations group, with a career spanning engineering and mathematics at MIT, law practice, and fintech risk management. He joined Chime in 2018, drawn by the mission to serve underserved populations, and his teams address risk management, member support, and data solutions. Brian championed Chime's Chicago office expansion and serves as executive sponsor for OutChime.
