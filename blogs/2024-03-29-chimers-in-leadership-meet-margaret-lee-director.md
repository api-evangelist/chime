Margaret Lee, Director of Lifecycle Marketing and CRM, joined Chime for its people and welcoming culture, with a path to leadership that began with six years and annual promotions at her first employer. She became a manager at twenty-five and emphasizes balancing detailed work understanding with high-level strategy. Margaret values cross-functional collaboration and celebrates diverse representations of leadership as a woman of color advancing in tech.
