Chime handles a lot of data — petabytes of data — on behalf of our members. We use the data to make decisions about things like fraud detection and prevention, bill processing, and SpotMe overdraft protection , to name just a few use cases. Part of helping to ensure that our members have financial peace of mind entails being good stewards of company resources.
