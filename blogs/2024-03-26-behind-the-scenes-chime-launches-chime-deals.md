Chime Deals provides members with cashback rewards on everyday purchases through partnerships with over thirty thousand merchant locations. Members activate location-based deals via the app and receive cashback within ten business days. The feature resulted from member research identifying demand for easy savings, with development spanning business development, security, compliance, marketing, engineering, and operations teams.
