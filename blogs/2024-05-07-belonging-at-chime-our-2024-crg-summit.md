Chime's Diversity, Equity, and Belonging team organized the company's inaugural three-day CRG Summit to support employee resource group leaders, featuring sessions on leadership development, capacity building, and strategic planning. Facilitators included internal Chime experts and external speaker Maceo Owens. Several CRGs have already implemented learnings, including collaborative initiatives and new engagement features.
