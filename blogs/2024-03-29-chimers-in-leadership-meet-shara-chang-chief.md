Shara Chang, Chief Compliance Officer, became the first lawyer in her family and has championed diversity in legal and compliance fields. She advocates for authenticity in leadership, drawing inspiration from the book "A Peacock in the Land of Penguins," and leads with transparency, listening before speaking and mentoring others. Her approach brings human experiences into compliance work.
